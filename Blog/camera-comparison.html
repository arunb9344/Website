Understanding CCTV Camera Types

The right CCTV camera in Chennai depends on your property type, lighting conditions, and surveillance needs. Here’s a breakdown of popular camera types:

  • Analog Cameras:Security Purpose: Analog Cameras are wired Cameras mostly used in 90% of Residentials, Small to medium Offices, Shops etc., It is Cost Effecient and reliable. Analog Cameras are comes in Hikvision, Dahua, CPPlus, Prama, Honeywell and more local brands. Most of the Analog Cameras comes with 2MP & 5MP. Local Manufacturers can build 3MP Cameras which will be equal to Branded 2MP Cameras. Can view in both Monitor or TV and Mobile.
  • Network Cameras:Security Purpose: Network Cameras are costlier. Its Quality and clarity will be Very Nice. Network Cameras are used in Large Companies like IT Companies, Large Factories and even it will used in some Residentials, Medium Offices. Network Camera Maintenance is low compared to Analog Cameras, in which you need to change connectors atleast per year. These are comes with 2MP, 4MP, 6MP, 8MP. Network Cameras are comes in Hikvision, Dahua, CPPlus, Prama and even in local Manufacturing brands.Can view in both Monitor or TV and Mobile.
  • WiFi Cameras:Monitoring Purpose: WiFi Cameras are used in Residential with 1 to 2 cameras requirement. Mostly Wifi cameras are recommended only for monitoring purpose like babies and aged peoples. It needs WiFi connection in your premise. It is not recommended for Security Purpose. Because Memory Card will be in Camera itself. If the camera was stolen, you wont get any playback footages.WiFi Cameras are only accessible via Mobile.
  • 4G Cameras:Monitoring Purpose.4G Cameras also used to monitor babies and aged persons. In this, 4G Sim card will be inserted in the Camera to access remotely.4G Cameras are only accessible via Mobile. Recommended only for 1 Camera Requirement. If more than 1 camera requirement, you need to have that much sim card and recharge will be waste.
  • Solar Cameras:Security Purpose: Solar Cameras are likely 4G Cameras. Solar Cameras are used in Farm House, Agriculture Land where there is no EB Power. Solar Cameras are powered with Solar. In this also 4G Sim Card will be used for Internet. This can be used for Security Purpose by purchasing Cloud Storage. This cameras are used where there is no other way. Streamings will have some latency.

Camera Features:

    All Analog, Network, WiFi, 4G, Solar Cameras has below functions in all brand.

  • Black & White Cameras: In Day Time, Cameras will be in Colour. But in Night time, Infrared Light in Camera gives Black & White view.
  • Black & White Cameras with Mic: In Day Time, Cameras will be in Colour. But in Night time, Camera view will be in Black & White. It has Mic in the Camera. If anyone near camera speaks, we can hear. In Outdoor, voice may be noisy.
  • Full Colour Cameras: In this Cameras, both day & Night view will be in Color. In Night time, LED light will ON. Its like Torch in Mobile. We cant control that light. In dark, camera light will glow automatically. This camera also has Mic in the Camera. If anyone near camera speaks, we can hear. In Outdoor, voice may be noisy.
  • Dual Light Cameras: In this Cameras, there are both Infrared Light and LED Light in Camera. It has 3 modes. 1st mode:Day Colour & Night Colour(LED Light will Glow at Night).2nd Mode: Day Colour. Night Black & White(Infrared Light will ON).3rd Mode: Day Colour. Night Normally it will be in Black & White. If any motion detected, LED Light will glow and we will have Colour View. This sudden Light glow will alert strangers.This Camera also has Mic in the Camera. If anyone near camera speaks, we can hear. In Outdoor, voice may be noisy.

Different types of Lens

  • Fixed Lens Camera:Available in all Analog, Network, WiFi, 4G, Solar Cameras. In this type of Lens Camera, view is fixed. It has 3.6mm Lens, 6mm Lens, 8mm Lens, 12mm Lens. Mostly used cameras are 3.6mm. If we have more than 20m passage like area, we can go for 6mm, 8mm. Increaseing mm in lens, will have long distance coverage. At the same time, nearby coverage will be less while increasing in lens mm. For Ex: 3.6mm Lens from fixed position, 2FT will be out of focus(Blind spot). In 6mm lens, 4Ft from fixed position will be blind spot. We can zoom the video digitally in Live & Playback. Digital zoom is pixelate.
  • Varifocal Lens Camera:Available in only Network Cameras. In this type of Camera, lens will be adjustable. We can Zoom optically in Live view. But, in playback we can do only digital zoom which will be pixelate. So, if we need to cover the gate only, we can zoom to that gate view in Live. SO that we can view the gate view clearly in playback.

Different types of Cameras

  • Fixed View Camera:Available in all Analog, Network, WiFi, 4G, Solar Cameras. In this type of Camera, view is fixed. We cant Pan, Tilt(Rotate) the camera. We can up, down and change the view only manually
  • PTZ Cameras:PT available in WiFi, 4G, Solar Cameras, Analog(Recently Launched by Hikvision). PTZ Available in only Network Cameras. In this type of Camera, we can rotate the camera. Mostly WiFi Cameras, 4G Cameras, SOlar Cameras comes with this PT Function. Now(2015), Hikvision launched PT Cameras in Analog Cameras, Network Cameras.In PTZ, Z referred to Optical Zoom. PTZ Cameras comes in Network Cameras in which Zooming Capacity is 10 times higher than Varifocal Camera.
