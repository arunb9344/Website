About Eye Tech Securities

At Eye Tech Securities, we are dedicated to providing cutting-edge CCTV solutions to protect homes, businesses, and communities across Chennai. Since our inception in 2018, we have grown to become a trusted name in security, known for our expertise, reliability, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Journey Since 2018

Founded in Madipakkam, Chennai, Eye Tech Securities began with a vision to enhance safety through advanced surveillance technology. Over the years, we have installed thousands of CCTV systems, serving a diverse range of clients, from small households to large commercial establishments. Our growth is a testament to our dedication to quality and trust, built one satisfied customer at a time.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission: To deliver state-of-the-art CCTV solutions that provide peace of mind, ensuring the safety and security of our clients’ properties.
Vision: To be the leading security solutions provider in Chennai, setting the standard for innovation, reliability, and customer-centric service in the industry.
